## Variables
> ### Type Declaration Characters
```vba
Sub Legacy()    ' Requires Implicit Declaration - No 'Option Explicit'
    a% = 1      ' % Integer
    b& = 2      ' & Long
    c! = 3      ' ! Single
    d# = 4      ' # Double
    e$ = "5"    ' $ String
    f@ = 6      ' @ Currency
    
    Debug.Print a & b & c & d & e & f
End Sub
```
